How to Submit?

Please submit your paper as the system opens (From 2020/9/15 to 2020/10/15):
1.Please use latex to format your paper.
2.Please submit your paper as a pdf file.
3.Please configure your file Name according to the following rule: T[No.]_[Name of the Author]_Abstract (No. : subject session number).
4. Please upload the abstract at the time of registration (click here to enter the registration system). If you need to update, please send the file to : tms2020@math.fju.edu.tw before October 31.
