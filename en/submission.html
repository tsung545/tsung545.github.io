Abstract Submission

Please submit your abstract through Registration System (on-line during September 15 - October 15, 2020) and follow the instructions below:
1.Use LaTeX to format your paper.
2.Submit your abstract as a PDF file.
3.Configure your file name as the following:
T[No.]_[Author Name]_Abstract
Note that [No.] refers to the corresponding session number, e.g. [5], [11], etc.
4.If you need to update the file, send it to: tms2020@math.fju.edu.tw by October 31, 2020.
